A little bit about me.

Hi! I'm Cindy. I'm a student in my 3B Mechatronics Engineering term at the University of Waterloo. I've done five co-op terms (or internships) so far, ranging from web design to mechanical and, of course, mechatronics engineering. My favourite co-ops are the ones at which I've stretched my problem-solving skills and contributed to a great product. I'm always looking for opportunities where I can continue to make meaningful work!

In my spare time, I create. Whether that means making art, a robot, or designing some apartment signs for a mutual friend, creating is in my bones. For the future, I look forward to expanding towards the product development industry.

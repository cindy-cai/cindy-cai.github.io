ABOUT

Hi! I'm Cindy. I'm in my 2B term in Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I've had three co-op terms so far, and have worked in web development/design, software development, and QA. I'd love to experience a more design-centric internship and am particularly interested in UI/UX, product design, and industrial design.

Having enrolled in art classes since the age of 7, I've loved visual arts for as long as I can remember. However, I've always enjoyed challenges and problem solving, and when I became really interested in mechanical/industrial design and robotics in high school, I knew I wanted to pursue an engineering degree. At the same time, my passion for the visual arts has never faded and drives me to continuously improve as an artist and designer.

Although being a full-time student takes up a lot of time, whenever I can, I'm drawing, developing, or designing my next personal project. I'm an avid learner and I pay a lot of attention to detail, which fuels my enthusiasm when it comes to putting my best work forward.
